    I am a passionate and dedicated individual transitioning from an 8-year career in education to the exciting world of front-end development. With a B.Ed. in Intermediate and Senior Phase Education, I’ve spent nearly a decade cultivating skills in communication, problem-solving, and user-centered thinking—skills that now guide my approach to designing and building engaging digital experiences.

    Over the past year, I’ve been actively developing my front-end skill set through hands-on practice, online courses, and personal projects. I’ve gained experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m currently building my portfolio website to showcase what I’ve learned and the projects I’ve completed. Along the way, I’ve also developed a strong interest in UI and UX design, focusing on how visual clarity, responsiveness, and usability contribute to the overall user experience.

My goal is to become a full-time front-end developer and collaborate with others who care about building beautiful, functional, and accessible websites. I’m excited to keep learning, improving, and contributing to the tech community through real-world projects and continuous growth.
